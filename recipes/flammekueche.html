Flammekueche recipe

A little bit of history

Flammekueche, or tarte flambée, is a speciality of the region of Alsace. It is composed of bread dough rolled out very thinly in the shape of a rectangle or oval, which is covered with fromage blanc or crème fraîche, thin-sliced onions and lardons. It is one of the most famous specialties of the region.

Ingredient list

Dough:

  • 250gr of all purpose flour
  • 18cl of water
  • 3 tbsp of oil
  • 1 tsp of salt

Toppings:

  • 20cl of creme fraiche
  • 200gr of lardons
  • 1 onion
  • Salt, pepper

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven at 230C.
  2. First, prepare the dough by kneading all the ingredients together with your hand until the dough is soft and non-sticky.
  3. Wrap the dough in clingfilm and let it rest in the frigde.
  4. In a pan, fry the lardons until they are cooked. Add some salt and pepper. Then put them in a plate without removing the fat they released from the pan.
  5. Cut the onion in slices*.
  6. Fry the onion in the same pan you cooked the lardons in without addind more fat.
  7. When the onions' colour turns lighter, remove them from the pan and put them with the cooked lardons. Stir the two ingredients together.
  8. Remove the dough from the fridge and roll out the dough on a floured table until it is thin.
  9. Lay the dough on parchment paper then spread the creme fraiche on it. Lastly, scatter the lardons mix on the dough.
  10. Put the tarte in the oven for 10 minutes.
  11. Enjoy your hard work.

* Pour cold water on the onion and the knife if you want to avoid tearing up too much.
